Bolts flared from Andars outstretched hands in a blinding wave.  Whereupon his lightning touched, the resultant infernoes did not merely light- they erupted. Each rock and tree, every leaf, every blade of grass bellowed out as if straining heretofore against some unseen bond.  Ever vigilant to keep their latent power, sealed across the ages, from undoing creation itself, at last they are relieved. 

The battleworn soldiers barely flinched at their doom.  For months, their eventual deaths had been more real to them than any prospect of waking the next morning at all.  The lighting scattered across the ranks, and the bodies burst into a red, smothering mist.
